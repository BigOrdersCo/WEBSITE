Our Mission


BigOrders is a tech startup founded by university students from MIT, Calvin University, and Penn State, that aims to make it easy for large organizations to support small businesses. We believe in the value of bringing humanity into consumerism in order to increase connection in today's tech-forward world. Our hope is to transform the system and the status quo, so that access doesn't have to compete with aptitude, especially for aspiring business owners.

Our mission is to make it easy for everyone to support small businesses, bring humanity into consumerism, and transform the system and the status quo.
