For Teams


Are you an organization or club looking to order food for your team? BigOrders allows you to place group orders with ease and support local small business restaurants.

  1. Select the date and time you want your order made for, a partnering restaurant, and your budget.
  2. Have your team place orders with your code.
  3. When everyone has placed their orders, review the group order and submit.
  4. Pay with your company card.
  5. Then, just send someone over to pick up your meal.

For Individuals


As an individual, you'll place an order with the restaurant your team chooses. First-time users take the Taste Palette Quiz, which is used to create your personalized taste palette profile. We'll take into account any dietary restrictions you have. Afterwards, we'll recommend dishes we think you'll like, and you can place your order. All done!

For Restaurants


If you're a small business restaurant interested in receiving group orders from companies and organization teams hassle-free, BigOrders can help. Create a restaurant profile with BigOrders, upload your menu, and we'll work with you to get you on our platform and receiving big orders from local teams.
